1. Project Vision

Veritas Forensics (Veridex) aims to be the definitive platform for digital trust and safety. By
providing multi-modal analysis of content (text, image, audio), it empowers researchers, journalists,
and legal teams to verify origin, detect bias, and ensure compliance in an AI-saturated digital
landscape.

3. Feature Breakdown

3.1. Multi-Modal Analysis

      Text Analysis: Direct input, URLs, or document uploads (PDF, DOCX).
      Image Analysis: Static image uploads (JPEG, PNG, WebP) with EXIF data extraction.
      Audio Analysis: Multi-format audio support with automated transcription for sentiment
          analysis.

3.2. Origin Detection

      AI vs. Human Classification: Probabilistic scoring of content origin.
      Model Fingerprinting: Identifying specific source models (e.g., Midjourney v6, GPT-4o,

          Flux, DALL-E) based on digital artifacts.

3.3. Bias & Sentiment Analysis

      Bias Mapping: Detection of political leaning, racial/gender stereotypes, and linguistic tropes.
      Sentiment Polarity: Granular breakdown of emotional tone and intent.

3.4. Fact-Checking & Veracity

      RAG Integration: Cross-referencing claims with real-time news APIs and trusted
          fact-checking databases (e.g., Snopes, Google Fact Check Explorer).

      Claim Extraction: Automatically identifying "atomic" claims in text for individual verification.

3.5. Copyright & Plagiarism

      Digital Fingerprinting: Similarity search against massive datasets to identify potential
          copyright infringements.

      Source Attribution: Mapping identified plagiarized segments to their original sources.
